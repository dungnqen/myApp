Socket button head cap screws are mostly used when the need for reducing the size of equipment arises. The critical characteristic feature of these screws is the wide load bearing surface. This particular quality makes it resistant to looseness. They are mostly used in the designing of compartments in the overhead space of a house. An essential expectation from those spaces is the ability to provide more space with limited hardware. To assist in the better assembly of all the parts, these screws also come with a wide cross-section.
